Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 05, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Crimson Desert Odds Calculator ("the App") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better experience for our users. This includes:

  • Usage Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect information about how you interact with the app (e.g., game modes selected, screens viewed).
  • Stability & Performance: We use Firebase Crashlytics and Performance Monitoring to collect crash reports and performance telemetry to help us fix bugs and improve speed.
  • Local Storage: We store your app settings and calculation history locally on your device using web storage. This data never leaves your device.

2. Advertising and Cookies

The App displays advertisements using Google AdMob. Google and its partners use cookies, unique device identifiers (such as Android AdID), and other local storage technologies to serve personalized ads based on your use of this App and other sites on the Internet.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom (UK), and Switzerland: We request your consent before using cookies, local storage, or personal data to serve personalized advertisements. You can manage or revoke your consent at any time by opening the App Settings and tapping the "REVOKE AD CONSENT" button.

3. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard services (Google Firebase) to handle telemetry data, which is transmitted over encrypted connections.

4. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal identifiers like names, emails, or phone numbers, we cannot identify specific users. Telemetry data is aggregated and anonymized.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
